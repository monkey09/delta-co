company profile

Company Name:

  • The Company founded on February 1999 emanations from El-Nasr company for Fertilizer and chemical Industries (Suez / Talkha), which had been established in 1946. Now The Company named EL DELTA COMPANY FOR FERTILIZERS AND CHEMICAL INDUSTRIES (ASMEDA).
  • The Company exists on the Demiatta Nile branch at about 120 km from Cairo.

The Company Plants are:

TALKHA (I):

was transplanted to its existed site after the 1967 war and was commissioned and startup in 1975, it produces Ammonium Nitrate 33.5% N.

TALKHA(II):

is a newer fertilizer train commissioned and starts in 1980,its produces prilled urea 46.5%N.(1725 ton/day).

CO-GENERATION Unit:

commissioned and started in May 2000, It produces 17.7 Mw/Hr; It's overall thermal cycle efficiency is 85.4%; Kwatt production cost is 0.032 L.E. ; And the Saved energy price /Year according to the last price is 34.3 MM. L.E.

PLASTIC BAGGING PLANT:

for producing heavy duty polyethylene bags from LDP& LLDP with a capacity of 33 Million bags/Year.

The legal form of company:

Joint stock company in Egypt - a Chemical Industries (LLC. M. S. M), denoted by the symbol (u.T. M. M).

The authorized capital:

200 million pounds.

Paid-Up Capital:

130 million pounds.

Company activities:

Chemical industry and in particular chemical fertilizers and chemicals associated with or derived from or for the industry as well as direct all operations related to the particular purpose or brokerage said. And have the right to buy and sell and carry out import and export business and commercial agencies on behalf of another and represents foreign companies.

historical events

  • 1946:

    Company was established by royal decree in August 1949 publication of the facts of Egyptian Annex No. 88 issued on 5/9/1946 with a capital of four million pounds, for the production of nitrogen fertilizers from New oil refineries, and limestone.

  • 1961:

    Nationalized Company partly 20/7/1961 under Act 119 of 1961 and then devolved to the entire state of 8/8/1963 under Presidential Decree No. 72 of 1963.

  • 1964:

    The Company contracted to construct a plant fertilizer ammonium nitrate lime 26, 5% card 380 thousand tons annually, and tests began operating in early 1967, as a result of the aggression in 1967 and then partially factories stopped completely in 1968.

  • 1970:

    Talkha location was chosen to re-establish plant ammonium nitrate 26, 5 nitrogen.

  • 1974:

    Been contracted to set up a factory for the production of urea 46,5%, and started production in October 1980 was also set with a factory to produce plastic raw polyethylene to produce bags for the mobilization of all fertilizers has started production in late 1979.

  • 1975:

    In August, appeared to produce ammonium nitrate fertilizer 26, 5% - in June 1976 have been converted to 31% of manure nitrogen - In March 1988 there has been shifting production to 33, 5% nitrogen.

  • 1992:

    Been contracted to set up a unit to produce Methanol capacity of 100 tons / day instead of a solution of copper pollution of the environment in Talkha (1).

  • 1994:

    Has been modified basket catalyst for ammonia solution Talkha (2) of the AXIAL FLOW RADIAL FLOW.

  • 1996:

    Been contracted to create a co-generation Talkha (2) to generate around 17, 3 MW / hour.

  • 1997:

    Pans have been modified to the type of urea reactors newer and more efficient.

  • 1999:

    Were separated and named the Delta Company for Fertilizers and Chemical Industries is licensed with a capital of hundred million pounds, and select the company's issued capital of $ 44 million.

  • Is considered to be the biggest project for energy conservation by implementing the co-generation technology in Egypt "self financed".
  • Project design, delivery, supervision, commissioning and conducting performance tests is executed by ROLLS-ROYS (U.K).
  • Civil construction is executed by (Arab Contractors, North Delta Branch).
  • Supervision and 3_rd part witness: Electrical power systems engineering company (EPS).
  • Total investments 66MM.L.E
  • Simple pay back period 4.4 Years
  • Total equipment values: 45MM.L.E
    • Mechanical equipment (represents 45%) 20.25MM.L.E
    • Elect. Equipment (represents 40%) 18.00MM.L.E
    • I. & C. (Represents 15%) 6.750MM.L.E
  • Generated power 17.7Meg.Watt
  • Saved energy / year (Which present about 40% of the total energy demand.) 140MM.k.w/h.

plastic bagging plant

Plastic plant vital sections of the company where plastics are manufactured Sacks heavy duty of raw polyethylene, low density and normal for the mobilization of both types of urea fertilizer 46.5% Azot, ammonium nitrate fertilizer, "enhanced Deltafert of 33.5% urea fertilizers, ore is mixed with polyethylene, polyethylene normal rough Linear rates specific to improve the specification, which bear sacks plastic packaging, loading, transport and aging.

plastic plant component of 6 lines with line feed extrusion welding automatic and also the oxidative stress Alaozhutiser to activate the film surface to accept printing ink and having data printing Aklashih on the sack surface and there are also cooling units for the film extrusion line, equipped with a printing machine as well as rolling machine.

plant equipped with air compressor to raise the crude within Alsellohat as well as the use of air pressure in the manufacturing process and production.

There are number 2 Sailo factory packaging 50 tons and is withdrawn the crude from Alsellohat to automatic feeding line by pushing compressed air.

The plant equipped with number 4 lines welding machines for cutting and welding of plastic bags produced specific lengths by type of fertilizer packaged.

Plant has public administration and maintenance including maintenance, electrical and mechanical work for the periodic maintenance of the plant.

Employment in the number of factory workers distributed 75 normal labor and technicians.

Average production of the factory bags / 6 lines extruders 31.5 million bags per year, and this production is linked to the plan of mobilization programs target of production and the proportion of faults during the operation.
